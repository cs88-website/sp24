Regrade requests

All regrade requests (aside from exam regrade requests) should be directed to Soumya, who is the TA in charge of grading. You can contact him at soumyab@berkeley.edu with the following information:

  • Your 61A login (e.g. cs61a-aa)
  • Your partner's name and login (if applicable)
  • The assignment you would like to be regraded
  • A link to your assignment submission on okpy.org
  • A brief description of your situation. In particular, please specify which question(s) you would like regraded.

NOTE: Please submit regrade requests for an assignment only after grades have been posted!

Grading policies

  • After homework 1, any homework or project that cannot load into Python will most likely get a zero. This includes, but is not limited to, files that result in SyntaxErrors and ImportErrors.
